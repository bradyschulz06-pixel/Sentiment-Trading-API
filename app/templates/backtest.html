Measure The Model

Runs on the current large-cap universe with daily closes, execution friction, and stricter exits.

This replay uses earnings-only scoring (PEAD). News sentiment is intentionally excluded — historical news reconstruction would produce misleading results. Exits are a hard stop at 7% below entry and a time stop at max hold days.
What This Replay Does
Uses daily Alpaca bars for price history and compares the strategy against {{ settings.backtest_benchmark_symbol }}.
Lets you test different stock universes and signal thresholds against the same earnings-only model.
Applies a market regime filter: new longs are blocked when SPY is below its 50-day SMA.
Applies historical earnings surprises only after the reported date is known.
Adds commission and slippage so paper-trading decisions are held to a tougher standard.
Uses a hard stop (7% below entry) and a time stop at max hold days — no trailing stop or profit-take.
