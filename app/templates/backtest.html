What This Replay Does
Uses daily Alpaca bars for price history and compares the strategy against {{ settings.backtest_benchmark_symbol }}.
Lets you test tighter stock universes and different momentum, sentiment, and earnings mixes with the same exit shell.
Applies a market regime filter so weak index trend or weak breadth blocks new longs and mixed tape raises the entry bar.
Applies historical earnings surprises only after the reported date is known.
Adds commission and slippage so paper-trading decisions are held to a tougher standard.
Uses hard stop, armed trailing stop, time stop, profit-take, and signal-fade exits.
